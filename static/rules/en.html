Shed the points in your hand, then call Stop when you think your hand total is strictly lower than every other active player. Keep your cumulative score below the cap, or you're eliminated!

🃏 Card values

Suits don't matter. Ace = 1 (strictly low) · numbers = face value · Jack = 11 · Queen = 12 · King = 13.

🖐️ On your turn — pick one

  • Discard — throw any 1 card, then draw 1.
  • Pair — throw 2 cards of the same rank, then draw 1.
  • Set — throw 3 or 4 cards of the same rank (no draw).
  • Sequence — throw 3+ cards in a consecutive run, e.g. A-2-3 (no draw).
  • Match — throw any cards matching the rank(s) currently in the center pile (no draw).

🛡️ Going to zero (Safe Zone)

Empty your hand and your round score locks at 0 points. You are now completely Safe and sit out the rest of the round.

🛑 Calling Stop (Ending the round)

Only at the start of your turn, and never during the first orbit. If your total is strictly lower than all other active players, you win and get a score discount. If any active player ties or beats you, you take a heavy penalty.

⚠️ Safe Player Exemption: Players who are Safe (0 points) are ignored during a Stop calculation. You only compete against players still holding cards!
