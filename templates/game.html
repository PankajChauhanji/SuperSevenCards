      How to play Super Seven

      Shed the points in your hand, then call Stop when you think you are lowest. Lowest cumulative score survives; cross the max and you are out.

      Card values

      Ace = 1 · numbers = face value · Jack = 11 · Queen = 12 · King = 13.

      On your turn — pick one

      • Discard one card — then draw one from the deck.
      • Pair — throw two of the same rank, then draw one.
      • Set — throw 3 or 4 of the same rank; no draw.
      • Sequence — throw 3+ cards in a run (suits don't matter); no draw.
      • Match — throw any cards whose ranks are showing in the center pile; no draw needed.

      Going to zero

      Empty your hand and your round score locks at 0 — you are safe and sit out the rest of the round.

      Calling Stop

      Only at the start of your turn, and never during the first orbit. If you are strictly lowest you win the round (your score drops by the win discount). If anyone ties or beats you, you take a heavy penalty on top of your hand.
